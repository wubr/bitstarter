Know before you leave for work that there's a train blocking your commute, thanks to some of the people already in line. Catch that double rainbow in the sky behind your house thanks to a neighbor. Keep the kids inside because someone in town caught sight of a bear in the last hour.

Be in the know and pass information along to others with See.Spot. for Android.

See.Spot. uses your phone's location and the information you enter about what you see, hear, or smell in order to get the word out to others. Spot traffic incidents, weather events, public safety hazards, or other interesting happenings and See.Spot. will tell others in the area who have expressed interest in the type of thing you're experiencing.
